How It Works

1. Farmers List Products

Farmers add available products with quantity, location, and availability date.

2. Buyers Search & Contact

Bulk buyers search nearby farmers and directly contact them.

3. Offline Coordination

Buyer and farmer coordinate delivery or pickup as per mutual convenience.

Who Can Use This Platform?

Farmers

List your produce, avoid middlemen, and connect directly with local buyers.

Bulk Buyers

Discover farmers, plan bulk purchases in advance, and get better pricing.
